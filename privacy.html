Privacy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Paysavr isn't launched yet. Right now, this website and its waitlist are all there is — so this policy is short, and every word of it is true.

What we collect

  • Your email address — because you emailed us to join the waitlist. That's the only way to join.
  • Your name, only if you include it in your email. It's optional.

That is the complete list. Joining the waitlist means your email sits in our support inbox like any other message.

What we don't collect

  • No financial data of any kind — no bank accounts, no statements, no transactions. The waitlist has nothing to do with your money.
  • No analytics, no tracking pixels, no cookies set by us, and no third-party scripts on this site. The pages you're reading are static files.
  • No data purchased from, or shared with, anyone.

How we use your email

  • To tell you when Paysavr opens and to offer you early access at the founding price.
  • To reply if you asked us something.

We won't sell your address, share it, or add you to anything else. Expect very few emails — there isn't much to say until launch.

Leaving the waitlist

Email hello@paysavr.com with "remove me" (or anything to that effect) and we'll delete your messages and your address. Done.

Where your email lives

Our mailbox is hosted on Microsoft 365. Your message is protected by the same controls as any business email there, and only Paysavr's operators can read it.

When the product launches

The Paysavr product will have its own privacy policy, published before it handles any of your information, and it will be held to the same standard as this page: plain language, complete list, no surprises. Joining the waitlist never opts you into anything the product later does.

Contact

Anything unclear, or any request about your data: hello@paysavr.com.

Paysavr shows you information about your own recurring spending and options you may want to consider. It is not financial, investment, or tax advice.
